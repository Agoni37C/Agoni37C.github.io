Of course, tenderness is reserved for those who can make me happy

Cherish the romantic universe and the daily life

心里藏着小星星 心里才能亮晶晶

Little stars are hidden in my heart to shine

只有双向的喜欢才值得等待

Only two-way affection is worth the wait

遇见你的那天樱花开满南山

The day I met you, the cherry blossoms were all over Nanshan

说不定你也是别人心里偷偷藏起来的星光呢

Maybe you are also a star hidden in the hearts of others

Try your best to do everything including be happy

无论何时 我都愿向温柔投降

No matter when I surrender to tenderness

枕头里住满了发霉的梦 梦里住满了遥远的你

Pillow full of moldy dream dream full of distant you

有你的地方 天气晴朗 万物可爱

Where you are, the weather is sunny and everything is lovely

以前山是山 海是海 而今山川江海都是你

Before mountains are mountains and seas are now mountains, rivers, rivers and seas are you

非常容易饿 请用你的温柔喂饱我

Very hungry Please feed me with your gentleness
